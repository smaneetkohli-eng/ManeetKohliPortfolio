About Me

Who I Am

Outside of AI, data, and business, I’ve always believed that the best work comes from a life fully lived.

The gym keeps me grounded — discipline outside of work bleeds into everything else. Music has been therapy my whole life. I grew up in a family where it was always playing, my outlet is guitar, and I lead a bhangra dance team — performing at concerts and events, including recently at a Dallas Mavericks game. Honestly, there are more hours in my day where music is on than when it isn’t.

I love capturing things — photos, videos, moments. There’s something that genuinely moves me about the idea that something I see or feel right now can exist forever. Traveling and food feed that same curiosity. Every new place, every unfamiliar flavor — it reminds me why I work as hard as I do. So I can do more of it.

But above everything, it’s the people. Laughing with my family, being absolute hooligans with my friends, and slow, quiet moments with my girlfriend — those are the ones I hold onto most.
